Coleslaw

Description

This classic coleslaw recipe features crisp shredded cabbage and carrots coated in a creamy, tangy dressing, making it the perfect chilled side for sandwiches, barbecue, or fried dishes.

Ingredients

  • Â½ white cabbage, finely shredded
  • 1 large carrot, grated
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar or lemon juice
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Put the cabbage and carrot into a large bowl.
  2. In a small bowl, mix the mayonnaise and vinegar or lemon juice.
  3. Pour the dressing over the vegetables.
  4. Mix well until everything is coated.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Chill in the fridge for at least 30 minutes before serving.
