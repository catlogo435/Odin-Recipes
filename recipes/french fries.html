French fries

Image by Hans Benn from Pixabay

Description

Crispy oven-baked French fries made from fresh potatoes with just oil and salt. An easy and tasty side dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • ½ tsp salt
  • Optional: black pepper or paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (200°C fan).
  2. Peel the potatoes and cut them into thin sticks.
  3. Rinse the potatoes in cold water, then dry them well.
  4. In a large bowl, toss the potatoes with the vegetable oil, salt, and any optional seasonings (like black pepper or paprika) until evenly coated.
  5. Spread the potatoes in a single layer on a baking tray.
  6. Bake for 35–40 minutes, turning halfway through.
  7. Remove when golden and crispy. Season to taste.
