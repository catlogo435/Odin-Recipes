Baked beans

Image by Peggy Chai from Pixabay

Description

A simple homemade version of classic baked beans, cooked in a rich tomato sauce with onion and garlic. Perfect as a side dish or on toast.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 tin (400 g) chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tin (400 g) haricot beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tbsp tomato purée
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and cook for 5 minutes until soft.
  3. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Stir in the chopped tomatoes and tomato purée.
  5. Add the sugar, salt, and pepper.
  6. Add the beans and stir well.
  7. Simmer gently for 15–20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
